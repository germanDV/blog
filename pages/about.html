About

I write about software development topics that I find interesting or useful and that I would like to preserve for my own reference and/or I would like to share with fellow developers, with the hope they might help someone.

As a general disclaimer, I will say that when I share something, it's not necessarily the best or the only option to tackle a problem. I advise readers to focus on the ideas rather than the concrete implementation. The same goes for terminology, I'll do my best to use correct language, but it can get tricky sometimes. My hope is that if you focus on the ideas, try them out, test them and adjust them to your needs/liking, you get something out of this site.
